OUR PRODUCTS

ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)

ISHYIGA ERP is an application that is used in selling and in stock management, each part (SALES & STOCK) is composed of many features that interact together on the same central data. This was built for the purpose of being used by big companies (companies that might supply other companies). This is the combination of technology processes and procedures that oversee the monitoring and maintenance of stocked products, with the ability of generating reports, maintaining the balance of the stock, and other more details about the purchased products and sales in the organization.

NIKI

NIKI is a web based system of ISHYIGA which was built with the aim of facilitating businesses (Hotels, Bar Resto, SuperMarkets, Pharmacies and other different businesses ) to have a global identification of the item/product. Meaning, Items are created by following the required details of the item. (for example, when an item is being created it must have the Item commercial description, Item category, Manufacture, Business sector and other more required details). This adds to the fact that clients who want good and full detailed reports with enough information of the items, this system is sufficiently good for them. NIKI is integrated in all ISHYIGA systems to pull the items in a stock.

VSDC

This system contributes many features, including the following: Internal control mechanismS Inventory management toolS Essential basis for accountingS Transparent mechanism in business Transparent means of exchange of informationS Financial tax audit Insurance for security informing taxpayers Clearinghouse for information in real time between taxpayer and tax administrator
